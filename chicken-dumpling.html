Chicken Dumpling

Cameron Thompson

Ingredients
  • 1/4 cup Butter
  • 1/2 cup Chopped Onion
  • 2/3 cups Flour
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 can Condensed chicken broth
  • 1 cup Water
  • 2 1/2 cups Chicken, cubed
  • 1 1/2 cups Mixed Vegetables (thawed)
  • 1 cup Sour Cream
  • 1 can Pillsbury Grands Southern
  • Style Biscuits
  • - chopped parsley
  • - Paprika

 

Directions
  • Melt butter in large heavy skillet. Add onion; cook and stir until tender. Lightly spoon flour into measuring cup; level off. Add flour and salt to pot; stir until smooth. Add broth and water; cook until bubbly and thickened, stirring constantly. Stir in chicken, mixed vegetables and sour cream. Simmer over low heat until thoroughly heated.
  • Separate dough into 8 biscuits; cut each in half. Place biscuit halves on top of hot chicken mixture. Simmer, uncovered, 10 minutes.
  • Cover; simmer an additional 15-20 minutes or until biscuits are no longer doughy. Sprinkle with parsley and paprika.
