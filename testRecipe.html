Dill Oyster Crakers

Shari Thompson Lyons

Ingredients
  • 1 12oz. pkg. Oysterettes
  • 1 10oz. pkg. Hidden Valley Original Ranch Dressing Mix
  • 1/8 tsp. Garlic Powder
  • 2 tsp. Dill
  • 1/2 c. Oil

 

Directions
  • In a large bowl, mix garlic powder with ranch mix, then add oil.
  • Mix well with whisk
  • Stir in dill then oysterettes
  • Mix well with wooden spoon
  • When all covered, store overnight in refrigerator
