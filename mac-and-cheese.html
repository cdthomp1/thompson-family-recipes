Mac and Cheese

Cameron Thompson

Ingredients
  • 1 (16 oz) Package Elbow macaroni
  • 6 Tbsp Butter
  • 6 Tbsp Flour
  • 2 tbsp Mustard, Ground
  • 1.5 tsp Pepper
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 4 cups Milk
  • 6 cups Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 1.5 cups Panko bread crumbs
  • 1 tsp Paprika

 

Directions
  • Preheat oven 400â„‰. Cook macaroni in large pot as directed on package. Rinse under cold water.
  • Melt butter in same saucepan on medium heat. Sprinkle with flour and seasonings. Cook and stir 2 minutes or until well blended. Gradually stir in milk until smooth. Stirring constantly, cook 3 minutes or until sauce starts to thicken. Stir in cheese until melted and smooth. Remove from heat.
  • Add macaroni; toss gently to coat. Pour into greased 13x9 dish.
  • Mix panko and paprika; sprinkle evenly over the top.
  • Bake 25-30 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown on top. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
